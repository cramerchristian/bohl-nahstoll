Why Aviation?

Aviation as the pioneer of effective teamwork

Even though technical advancements were making airplanes safer and more reliable, in the 70`s realization started setting in that accidents in aviation were not diminishing. In more than 80% of the cases, accidents were found to have been caused by faulty teamwork.

The consensus was, that interpersonal competencies of the pilots had to be improved and fostered in order to achieve a higher and more effective level of teamwork. They were trained to make good decisions in time critical situations, to keep calm and to avoid decisive mistakes caused by bad teamwork. Since then the amount of accidents has been significantly reduced.

Various branches can profit from this success!

We will show you how to guide your business through challenging situations safely and successfully by using methods perfected in aviation.
